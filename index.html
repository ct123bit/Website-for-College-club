About Us

The Environment Protection and Awareness Club of BIT Mesra, or EPAC is the technical club of the college, whose sole motive is to instigate the students of the campus to proactively contemplate about the detrimental effects caused by human activities, on our environment. We at EPAC, as a student body aim at, improvising the existing systems which lay in the favor of nature. We have initiated a plethora of initiatives to retain the lush greenery of the campus and to create awareness among the students regarding the repercussions of an unhealthy environment. With an objective to nurture nature in return to what all it has done for us; we selflessly carry out activities with paramount dedication and unequivocal semblance of ideas to save what has been ours for so many years.
We believe change comes one step at a time, and this change can be brought upon by the young minds of the globe. Thus, we believe in bringing out the best from the students at the college, while developing them as responsible and aware inhabitants of Earth.
